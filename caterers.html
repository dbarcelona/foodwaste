Caterers and event teams

Partner with us before the trays leave the room.

A recovery plan helps caterers protect food quality, reduce waste, and offer clients a stronger sustainability option.

What caterers need

Caterers need a process that is fast, predictable, and safe. The recovery team should not slow breakdown, create unclear liability, or ask staff to make judgment calls without guidance.

What we can provide

  • Pre-event surplus planning with the client.
  • Acceptable food guidelines and packaging instructions.
  • Pickup windows that match event teardown.
  • Temperature and chain-of-custody records.
  • Partner feedback on what food types distribute well.
Before

Confirm estimated surplus, packaging needs, and pickup time.

During

Hold food safely, label trays, and separate donation-ready items.

After

Transfer food, log details, and send proof of recovery.

A reliable recovery partner can become a selling point for catered events.
