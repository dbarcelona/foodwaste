Companies ordering catering

Donate your food and get rewarded.

Turn leftover catered meals into a documented community benefit instead of a trash expense.

Why companies would sign up

  • Lower disposal waste after catered events.
  • Receive simple impact reports for internal sustainability storytelling.
  • Support local food access without asking employees to coordinate logistics.
  • Maintain records that may support California edible food recovery compliance.
  • Offer employees a visible way to connect office life with local community needs.

What the service provides

  • Scheduled or rapid-response pickups.
  • Food safety checklist, thermometer logs, and labeling guidance.
  • Recipient partner matching based on food type and capacity.
  • Monthly reporting on pounds, meals, and avoided waste.
  • Optional equipment kits for hot and cold holding.

Possible pricing
