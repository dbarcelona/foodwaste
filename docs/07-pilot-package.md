# Pilot Package

## Pilot name

Oakland Corporate Catering Recovery Pilot

## Pilot promise

Recover eligible surplus food from catered corporate events and deliver it to local food access partners with clear safety, documentation, and impact reporting.

## Pilot scope

### Included

- pre-event donor intake
- caterer staging instructions
- recipient matching
- pickup and direct delivery
- basic temperature and condition logging
- delivery confirmation
- short impact report

### Not included without separate agreement

- tax receipt issuance
- accepting homemade food
- storing food overnight
- guaranteeing recipient acceptance of all food
- accepting food with unknown safety history
- serving food directly to individuals
- legal compliance certification

## Ideal first pilot customer

- Oakland-based office, venue, or organization that orders catering at least twice per month
- willing to identify one internal pickup contact
- willing to pay for a small pilot or introduce a funder
- able to give advance notice when possible
- has leadership interest in food access, sustainability, or employee engagement

## Pilot pricing options

| Option | Price | Best for |
|---|---:|---|
| Single event pickup | $150 | One catered meeting or small event |
| Launch pilot | $750 | Up to 5 pickups in 60 days |
| Event recovery package | $1,500+ | Large event, conference, or multi-day program |
| Sponsored community route | $2,500+ | Company sponsors pickups from multiple local donors |

Pricing must be validated against actual route time, mileage, insurance, packaging, coordination labor, and failed pickup rates.

## Pilot success metrics

- 5 successful pickups
- 500+ pounds recovered or a validated smaller baseline
- 2+ recipient partners tested
- 80%+ recipient usability rate
- donor team reports low friction
- no food safety incidents
- documented repeat interest from donor or caterer

## Pilot operating assumptions

- Founder-led coordination
- Paid or founder-operated pickups at first
- No volunteer-only corporate commitment until SOPs are proven
- Direct-to-recipient delivery only
- No overnight storage in the first pilot
- Written donor and recipient terms before launch

## Pilot timeline

### Week 1

- confirm donor
- confirm caterer contact
- confirm recipient partners
- review food categories
- review insurance questions

### Week 2

- run dry-run intake
- prepare labels and logs
- confirm pickup route
- complete first pickup

### Weeks 3 to 6

- complete remaining pickups
- interview partners after each pickup
- refine acceptance policy

### Week 7

- produce impact report
- request testimonial or referral
- propose recurring plan
