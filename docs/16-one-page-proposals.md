# One-Page Proposals

## Company pilot proposal

### Food Recovery Oakland: Corporate Catering Recovery Pilot

Your catered events can feed neighbors instead of filling trash bags.

Food Recovery Oakland helps companies recover eligible surplus food from catered meetings and events and deliver it to local food access partners.

### What we handle

- donor intake
- caterer staging instructions
- recipient matching
- pickup and delivery
- food safety/condition logs
- delivery confirmation
- impact reporting

### Pilot structure

- 5 pickups over 60 days
- Oakland-first routing
- direct delivery to local partners
- no overnight storage
- unsafe or ineligible food refused

### Pilot price

Launch pilot: $750

### What you receive

- pickup confirmations
- end-of-pilot impact report
- lessons for recurring recovery
- optional internal story for employees

### Best fit

Companies with recurring catered meetings, trainings, or events and a desire to reduce waste while supporting local food access.

## Caterer partner proposal

### Make food recovery part of your event offering

Food Recovery Oakland gives caterers a responsible recovery partner for eligible surplus food after corporate events.

### Why partner

- offer clients a sustainability option
- reduce awkward leftover decisions
- protect staff from ad hoc donation requests
- keep teardown moving
- document the recovery

### What we need from caterers

- food category estimate
- pickup window
- packaging/staging cooperation
- point of contact
- honest refusal of unsafe food

### What we provide

- client-facing recovery language
- staging instructions
- pickup coordination
- delivery confirmation
- pilot feedback

## Recipient partner proposal

### Prepared food recovery support for Oakland partners

Food Recovery Oakland is testing a careful pickup service for eligible surplus food from corporate catered events.

### Our commitment

- no donation dumping
- no surprise deliveries without confirmation
- direct communication before each drop
- refusal rights
- food category screening
- feedback after each pickup

### What we need to learn

- what food is actually useful
- what times work
- what packaging is acceptable
- what quantities create burden
- how to confirm receipt simply

### Pilot ask

Participate in a short interview and consider receiving one to three controlled pilot deliveries if the food type and timing fit your operations.
