# B Impact Evidence Plan

This is a preparation guide for eventual Certified B Corp assessment. It should be updated against the live B Lab standards before applying.

## Governance evidence

Collect:

- benefit corporation formation documents
- mission statement
- public benefit purpose
- board minutes showing stakeholder impact discussion
- conflict-of-interest policy
- ethics policy
- annual benefit report draft
- decision framework for balancing profit and impact

## Workers evidence

Collect when employees or contractors exist:

- compensation policy
- contractor classification review
- worker safety policies
- anti-harassment policy
- training records
- driver safety procedures
- food safety training records
- benefits approach
- inclusive hiring plan

## Community evidence

Collect:

- local partner agreements
- recipient feedback
- Oakland-first procurement policy
- volunteer/advisor roster
- community needs interviews
- local hiring or contracting records
- partner usefulness scores
- donor education materials

## Environment evidence

Collect:

- pounds of food recovered
- estimated food waste avoided
- route mileage
- reusable packaging policy
- equipment reuse/rental plan
- waste hierarchy policy: feed people before compost
- greenhouse gas estimate methodology once selected

## Customers evidence

Collect:

- donor impact reports
- donor satisfaction surveys
- customer education materials
- food safety and refusal policy
- issue resolution records
- claims review process

## Disclosure evidence

Collect:

- public website claims
- source list for metrics
- policy caveats
- annual impact summary
- public benefit report

## Evidence tracker fields

- Evidence item
- B Impact category
- Owner
- Location
- Status
- Date created
- Review date
- Notes

## Early operating policies to adopt

- Food acceptance policy
- Donation refusal policy
- Partner respect policy
- Conflict-of-interest policy
- Data privacy policy
- Public claims policy
- Incident response policy
- Supplier standards
- Route emissions tracking policy
