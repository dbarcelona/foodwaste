# Route Day SOP

This SOP is for controlled pilots only. Review with food safety, insurance, and legal advisors before live use.

## Before leaving

- confirm donor contact
- confirm recipient contact
- confirm pickup window
- confirm delivery window
- confirm accepted food category
- check route time
- check vehicle cleanliness
- pack thermometer, labels, gloves, bins, logs, and sanitizer

## At donor site

1. Check in with donor or caterer contact.
2. Confirm food was intended for donation.
3. Inspect packaging and visible condition.
4. Confirm food type matches accepted categories.
5. Record temperature if required.
6. Refuse unsafe, unclear, or unwanted items.
7. Label accepted items if needed.
8. Load food into clean containers or insulated carriers.
9. Record pickup time, quantity, and notes.
10. Notify recipient that delivery is en route.

## During transport

- drive directly to recipient
- keep hot/cold controls as planned
- avoid unauthorized stops
- keep food covered and protected
- call recipient if delayed

## At recipient site

1. Check in with recipient contact.
2. Unload food.
3. Review food categories and quantity.
4. Ask recipient to confirm usable quantity.
5. Record refused or unusable items.
6. Capture signature/email/text confirmation.
7. Thank recipient and ask one feedback question.

## After route

- send donor confirmation
- update pickup log
- update KPI tracker
- note operational issues
- schedule follow-up if pilot partner

## Stop-work conditions

Stop or refuse pickup if:

- food appears contaminated
- food has unknown holding history
- donor contact cannot confirm basic details
- packaging is unsafe for transport
- recipient no longer wants/can accept the food
- route timing makes safe delivery doubtful
- driver feels unsafe
