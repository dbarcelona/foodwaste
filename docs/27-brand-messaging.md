# Brand Messaging

## Working positioning

Food Recovery Oakland makes food recovery easier than throwing good food away.

## Primary tagline options

- Share your food. Keep neighbors fed.
- Good food should not need a second chance.
- Catered surplus, recovered with care.
- From event trays to neighborhood tables.
- The missing handoff for surplus food.

## Messaging pillars

### Operational relief

We handle the pickup, partner matching, and documentation.

### Community impact

Surplus food becomes local support instead of landfill waste.

### Safety and trust

Food recovery works only when unsafe food is refused and partners know what to expect.

### Measurable outcomes

Donors receive practical impact reporting: pickups, pounds, estimated meals, and lessons.

## Words to use

- eligible surplus food
- recipient partners
- recovery route
- impact report
- food safety logs
- donor-side workflow
- Oakland-first
- direct delivery

## Words to avoid or use carefully

- waste, when speaking to donors about their food
- rescue, if it sounds savior-like in community contexts
- guaranteed tax deduction
- certified compliance
- zero risk
- all leftovers accepted
- charity, if the entity is for-profit

## Short website copy

Food Recovery Oakland helps companies recover eligible surplus food from catered events and deliver it to local food access partners. We handle the missing handoff: screening, pickup, recipient matching, delivery confirmation, and impact reporting.

## Corporate email opener

When catered food remains after an event, the question should not be whether anyone cares. It should be whether the handoff is ready.

## Recipient partner opener

We are designing this service around what food access partners can actually use, not what donors simply want to get rid of.
