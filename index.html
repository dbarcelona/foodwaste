The opportunity

Prepared food is often wasted because the handoff is hard.

Corporate meals and catered events can end with trays of safe, edible food but no simple recovery plan. This project is designed to handle the missing middle: pickup logistics, food safety records, partner matching, and impact reporting.

01

Recover

Pick up surplus food from offices, event venues, and caterers before it becomes waste.

02

Protect

Use temperature logs, clear labeling, safe packaging, and trained pickup protocols.

03

Redistribute

Deliver to local food pantries, shelters, community fridges, and neighborhood programs.

04

Report

Give donors simple records for meals shared, pounds recovered, and waste avoided.

Built for corporate excess

Not a restaurant discount app.

This concept focuses on high-volume moments where food is already prepared, already paid for, and likely to be discarded: catered trainings, client meetings, conferences, office lunches, and large private events.

The goal is to make donation easier than disposal while giving companies a credible way to support food access, climate goals, and California food recovery compliance.

For companies Donate your catered food and get rewarded with impact records. For caterers Partner with a recovery team that protects your food and your reputation. For neighbors Find Bay Area food access and volunteer opportunities in one place.

Global goals, local routes

Aligned with Zero Hunger and Responsible Consumption.

  • UN SDG 2: improve access to safe, nutritious, sufficient food.
  • UN SDG 12: reduce food waste and support more responsible consumption.
  • California SB 1383: help covered food donors establish agreements, recover edible food, and maintain records.
