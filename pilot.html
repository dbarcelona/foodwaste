Start here

Pilot one recovery route before building the whole system.

One donor, one caterer, one recipient partner, and five pickups can prove whether the model works.

What the pilot includes

  • Pre-event donor intake and food category screening.
  • Caterer staging instructions and pickup window confirmation.
  • Recipient matching before food leaves the event.
  • Pickup, delivery, temperature/condition logging, and delivery confirmation.
  • Post-pilot impact report with pounds, estimated meals, lessons, and next recommendation.
Week 1

Confirm donor, caterer, recipient partner, accepted foods, and insurance/legal review questions.

Weeks 2-6

Run five pickups, collect logs, refuse unsafe food, and interview partners after each route.

Week 7

Deliver the pilot report and decide whether to continue, expand, or redesign.
